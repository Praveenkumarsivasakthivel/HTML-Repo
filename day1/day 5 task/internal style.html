FULL STACK DEVELOPMENT

Many people ask, “How long does it take to become full stack developer?” The answer lies in the time you need to master the front-end and back-end skills. We have provided you with a breakdown of the necessary skills so that you have working knowledge regarding what to learn to become full stack developer. To advance your career in web development, enroll in courses in Web Development.

While HTML and CSS are the basic building blocks of web development, you also need to learn Angular or React, both of which are JavaScript based. Information about APIs is also required to solidify your foundation as a full-stack developer.
